Welcome to my research website

Hi, my name is David Desobry and I am a researcher in the computer graphics fields. My research interests include differential geometry and its application for hexaedral meshing with global parameterization techniques.

About me

I received my PhD from the University of Lorraine in 2023. I have published several articles in top-tier conferences and journals, and have given numerous talks at conferences and workshops.
